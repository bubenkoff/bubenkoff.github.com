COMFORT PASSES (VIP)
Comfort/VIP Passes grant entrance to the festival area as well exclusive access to the various Comfort zones at the festival. Comfort zones are located at the Mainstage as well as various other stages. You will experience the best views of the stages, a selection of hors d'oeuvres and the opportunity to purchase bottle-service. With the Comfort pass you receive parking on-site and speedy access.

MINIMUM AGE
The minimum age for entrance into the festival is 21. Government issued photo ID required.

HOW TO RECEIVE YOUR BRACELET
After finalizing your ticket purchase, you will receive an E-ticket. Print out this E-ticket & bring it to the TomorrowWorld/DreamVille Will Call desk where it will be exchanged for (a) bracelet(s). You cannot enter the festival without a bracelet. YOU MUST BRING PHOTO IDENTIFICATION AND THE CREDIT CARD USED TO PURCHASE THIS TICKET(S) TO THE "WILL CALL DESK" IN ORDER TO RECEIVE YOUR FESTIVAL BRACELET(S).
